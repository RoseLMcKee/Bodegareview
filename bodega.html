Illegal everything

If you find yourself at Cohlba Smoke Shop & Grill you have stepped into the past and made some mistakes. This bodega exclusively gets their goods delivered in the middle of the night by the shady dollar short buses that move up and down flatbush. You can buy ancient 40s imported from Canada and loosy cigarettes, but you must have the look of dishevelment that only comes from the endless bike ride from Sheapshead Bay you just took after the week long film shoot where you stay with your drug addled friend from Highschool in his squat apartment. You will only be welcome if you wonder in with a bit of unmark gravedirt on your pull overs. This place is for your last stop or if your really unlucky its your own personal Oregan Trail trade post.
